KUNAFA BOWL

KUNAFA IS MADE BY USING SHREDDED KATAIFI DOUGH, WHICH IS MIXED WITH MELTED BUTTER AND LAYERED IN A BAKING TRAY. A SWEET FILLING, USUALLY MADE OF SOFT WHITE CHEESE LIKE AKAWI OR RICOTTA, OR A CREAMY CUSTARD, IS SPREAD IN THE MIDDLE. ANOTHER LAYER OF DOUGH IS PLACED ON TOP. THE DESSERT IS THEN BAKED UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN AND CRISPY. AFTER BAKING, HOT SUGAR SYRUP INFUSED WITH ROSE OR ORANGE BLOSSOM WATER IS POURED OVER THE KUNAFA. IT IS GARNISHED WITH CHOPPED PISTACHIOS AND SERVED WARM. KUNAFA IS POPULAR IN THE MIDDLE EAST, ESPECIALLY DURING RAMADAN.
